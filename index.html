Eventful: event management by and for organizers

Elevator Pitch

Large events such as conferences, hackathons, and tournaments frequently utilize a large number of volunteers to staff. Staffing includes handling several errands of varying complexity and time-sensitivity, such as setting up venues, moving boxes, guiding attendees, etc. While the use of staffers is essential to the event running well, it causes several inefficiencies for organizers: it is difficult for an organizer to know which staffer is where, which staffers are free, and which are currently completing a task. Notifications from a general group messaging system are not targeted enough and can easily get lost, while individually contacting people is too tedious and time consuming for such time-sensitive tasks.

From our experiences organizing such events, we think that a dynamic platform for an organizer to manage these staffers would significantly optimize the event experience. We imagine one cohesive platform for the organizer to assign and track tasks to individuals effectively. The organizer can push tasks to a stack and assign them to staffers. By forcing a staffer to accept and decline tasks individually, we place more accountability on the staffer to get a task done. We also allow the organizer can also visually track which tasks are in progress, done, or not started all in one convenient platform.
